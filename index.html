Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right courses for me?

We offer personalized recommendations based on your interests and career goals. You can also browse course descriptions and reviews to find the best fit.

Do I get a refund if the course isn't worth my money?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you’re not satisfied with your course. Please refer to our Refund Policy for details.

What are the prerequisites for a course?

Prerequisites vary by course. Each course page lists the required skills or knowledge needed to enroll.

How is the course delivered?

Courses are delivered online through video lectures, interactive assignments, and downloadable resources.

Will you assist me to get my first job?

We provide career support including resume reviews, interview preparation, and job placement assistance for select programs.

What is the course curriculum?

Each course has a detailed curriculum outlining topics, learning objectives, and assessment methods. Check the course page for specifics.

What kind of support is available for students?

Our support team is available via email and live chat. You’ll also have access to discussion forums and peer groups.

What kind of certification does the course offer?

Upon completion, you’ll receive a certificate that you can share with employers and add to your professional profile.
